Classic Salmon Nigiri

Traditional Japanese nigiri sushi with fresh salmon slices over seasoned rice.

Prep time: 30 mins

Cook time: 15 mins

Servings: 2

Difficulty: Intermdiate

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 200g fresh sashimi-grade salmon
  • Wasabi paste
  • Soy sauce for serving
  • Pickled ginger for serving

Instruction

  1. Rinse the sushi rice until the water runs clear, then cook.
  2. Mix vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved.
  3. Fold vinegar mix into cooked rice and cool.
  4. Slice salmon thinly (about 1/4 inch thick).
  5. Wet hands, form rice into oval shapes (2 tbsp each).
  6. Add a small amount of wasabi on rice.
  7. Top with salmon and press gently.
  8. Repeat for remaining rice/salmon.
  9. Serve with soy sauce and pickled ginger.
